#Fairyground Variant Description File
#Syntax: <variant ID (delcared in variants.ini)>|<variant display name>|<variant description>
#The display name will be displayed in the variant dropdown as the item name.
#The description will be displayed in the dropdown as tooltips, you can hover your cursor on an item to view that.
#This can also be accessed from <About This Variant> button.

chess|Chess|Chess, unmodified, as it's played by FIDE standards.
crazyhouse|Crazy House|Take captured pieces and drop them back on to the board as your own.
atomic|Atomic Chess|Pieces explode upon capture, destroying all pieces except pawns in the 8 square around the destination square and itself.
kingofthehill|King Of The Hill|Bring your King to the center to win the game.
3check|3 Check|Check your opponent 3 times to win the game.
placement|Placement Chess|Choose where your pieces start.
duck|Duck Chess|The duck (a wall square that blocks any piece from moving to it) must be moved to a new square after every turn.
racingkings|Racing Kings|Hinder the opponent's king and make your king reach the 8th rank first to win. Checks are not allowed.
giveaway|Giveaway Chess|Lose all your pieces or get stalemated to win. Capturing is compulsory.
torpedo|Torpedo|Pawns can always move forward one or two squares.
nocastle|No Castling|Castling is not allowed.
pawnsideways|Pawns Sideways|Pawns can also move sideways one square.
knightmate|Knight Mate|The king moves like a knight while the knights move like a king.

makruk|Makruk|Thai Chess. A game closely resembling the original Chaturanga. Similar to Chess but with a different queen and bishop.
makpong|Makpong|Makruk variant where kings cannot move to escape out of check.
cambodian|Cambodian Chess|Makruk with a few additional opening abilities.
sittuyin|Sittuyin|Burmese Chess. Similar to Makruk, but pieces are placed at the start of the match.
asean|Asean|Makruk using the board/pieces from International Chess as well as pawn promotion rules.

shogi|Shogi|Japanese Chess, the standard 9x9 version played today with drops and promotions.
minishogi|Mini Shogi|5x5 Shogi for more compact and faster games. There are no knights or lances.
kyotoshogi|Kyoto Shogi|A wild Shogi variant on a 5x5 board where pieces flip into a different piece after each move.
dobutsu|Dobutsu|3x4 game with cute animals, designed to teach children how to play Shogi.
gorogoroplus|Gorogoro+|5x6 Shogi designed to introduce tactics with the generals.
torishogi|Tori Shogi|A confrontational 7x7 variant with unique pieces each named after different birds.
shoshogi|Sho Shogi|A new piece called Drunk Elephant is introduced, which can promote to a new king. You can lose one of the 2 kings after the promotion.

xiangqi|Xiangqi|Chinese Chess, one of the oldest and most played board games in the world.
manchu|Manchu Xiangqi|Xiangqi variant where one side has a chariot that can also move as a cannon or horse.
janggi|Janggi|Korean Chess, similar to Xiangqi but plays much differently. Tournament rules are used.
minixiangqi|Mini Xiangqi|Compact version of Xiangqi played on a 7x7 board without a river.

capablanca|Capablanca Chess|Play with the hybrid pieces, archbishop (B+N) and chancellor (R+N), on a 10x8 board.
capahouse|Capablanca House|Capablanca with Crazyhouse drop rules.
seirawan|Seirawan Chess|S-Chess. Hybrid pieces, the hawk (B+N) and elephant (R+N), can enter the board after moving a back rank piece.
shouse|Seirawan House|S-Chess with Crazyhouse drop rules.
grand|Grand Chess|Play with the hybrid pieces, archbishop (B+N) and chancellor (R+N), on a grand 10x10 board.
grandhouse|Grand House|Grand Chess with Crazyhouse drop rules.
shako|Shako|Introduces the cannon and elephant from Xiangqi into a 10x10 chess board.
shogun|Shogun|Pieces promote and can be dropped, similar to Shogi.
hoppelpoppel|Hoppel-Poppel|Knights capture as bishops; bishops capture as knights.
amazon|Amazon|Replace the Queen with Amazon (Q + N).
gothic|Gothic Chess|Gothic chess. Capablanca chess with better starting positon.
chaturanga|Chaturanga|Ancient form of chess. Pawns, bishops(elephants), and queen(ferz) are different from the ones in modern chess.

orda|Orda Chess|Asymmetric variant where one army has pieces that move like knights but capture differently.
synochess|Syno Chess|Asymmetric East vs. West variant which pits the western Chess army against a Xiangqi and Janggi-styled army.
shinobiplus|Shinobi+|Asymmetric variant which pits the western Chess army against a drop-based, Shogi-styled army.
empire|Empire Chess|Asymmetric variant where one army has pieces that move like queens but capture as usual.
ordamirror|Orda Mirror|Orda Chess variant with two Horde armies. The Falcon replaces the Yurt.
chak|Chak|Mayan chess. Inspired by cultural elements of Mesoamerica.
chennis|Chennis|Pieces alternate between two forms with each move.
spartan|Spartan|Asymmetric Spartans vs. Persians variant.

ataxx|ATAXX|Infection game. Pieces can turn the enemy pieces in the 8 squares around the destination square into friendly pieces after move.
snailtrail|Snail Trail|The squares that pieces have moved from will be blocked. The one who gets stuck loses.
horde|Horde|Lots of pawns VS chess army. White wins when checkmating black while black wins when capturing all white pieces.
